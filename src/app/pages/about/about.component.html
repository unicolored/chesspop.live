About

ChessPop is a fun, interactive way to watch chess games in 3D—like bringing the board to life right in your browser. Grab some popcorn, sit back, and enjoy the show!

If you’re enjoying Chesspop and want to support the project, you can sponsor it! Every little bit helps keep the chess popping!

Get in Touch

Chesspop uses a cool 3D view powered by Chessfield package.

Got ideas or feedback? Drop an issue on GitHub.

Built with love for chess lovers everywhere. Enjoy the games!
