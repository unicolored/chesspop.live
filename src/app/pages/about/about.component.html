About

ChessPop is a fun, interactive way to watch chess games in 3D—like bringing the board to life right in your browser. Grab some popcorn, sit back, and enjoy the show!

Why ChessPop?

I’m an online chess player myself, and I wanted a way to keep that real-life, over-the-board feel while watching games online. Chesspop uses a cool 3D view (powered by my Chessfield package) to help you see and analyze games in a fresh, spectator-friendly way. It’s all about enjoying chess like you’re right there at the table!

Get in Touch

Got ideas or feedback? I’d love to hear from you! Drop an issue on GitHub—it’s the easiest way to reach me. If you’re enjoying Chesspop and want to support the project, you can buy me a coffee—every little bit helps keep the chess popping!

Built with love for chess lovers everywhere. Enjoy the games!
